About Us

Beatz of Dairy Queens band is american dairy band formed in Nashville, Tennessee. The band is composed of Blizzard, Hot Fudge, Peach Caramel, Baby swiss and Butter. All band members belived in achieving dreams and not giving up on their passions. The Band's early music was southern rock and gradually evolved into Folk rock. The band achieved initial success and all three of the albums at the time peaked in the top five of US albums chart. The album Cow goes Moo was their first Platinum-selling album in the United States, and was also the best-selling album of 2017.The band's third Album, Treat your beats was released on July 2018. Beatz of Dairy Queens was awarded by Academy of Dairy and the new artist of year by Association of Dairy. In addition to being honored, Beatz of Dairy Queens has made efforts to recognize veterans who have returned from service and contributed to their communities.
